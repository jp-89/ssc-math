(i) $a^m ∙ a^n = a^{m + n} $
(ii) $a^m/a^n = a^{m - n} $
(iii)$ (a^m)^n = a^{mn} $
(iv)$ a = 1 (a ≠ 0). $
(v)$ a^{-n} = 1/a^n $
(vi)$ √^n{a^m} = a^{m/n} $
(vii)$ (ab)^m = a^m ∙ b^n. $
(viii)$ (a/b)^m = a^m/b^m $
(ix) If $a^m = b^m (m ≠ 0)$, then a = b. $
