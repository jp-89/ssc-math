● Geometrical Progression (G.P.) : (i) The general form of a G.P. is a, ar, ar², ar³, . . . . . where a is the first term and r, the common ratio of the G.P. (ii) The n th term of the above G.P. is t₀ = a.rn−1n−1 . (iii) The sum of first n terms of the above G.P. is S = a ∙ [(1 - rⁿ)/(1 – r)] when -1 < r < 1 or, S = a ∙ [(rⁿ – 1)/(r – 1) ]when r > 1 or r < -1. (iv) The geometric mean of two positive numbers a and b is √(ab) or, -√(ab). (v) a + ar + ar² + ……………. ∞ = a/(1 – r) where (-1 < r < 1).
