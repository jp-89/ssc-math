$ x = {√{2a+3b}+√{2a-3b}}/ {√{2a+3b}-√{2b-3b}} $

= $ {x+1} /{x-1}= {(√{2a+3b}+√{2a-3b})+(√{2a+3b}-√{2b-3b})}/ {(√{2a+3b}+√{2a-3b})-(√{2a+3b}-√{2b-3b})} $

বা, ${x+1} /{x-1}= {2(√{2a+3b})}/ {2(√{2a-3b})} $

বা, ${x+1} /{x-1}= {(√{2a+3b})}/ {(√{2a-3b})} $

উভয় পক্ষকে বর্গ করিয়া পাই
বা, ${(x+1)^2} /{(x-1)^2}= {(√{2a+3b})^2}/ {(√{2a-3b})^2} $

বা, ${x^2+2x+1} /{x^2-2x+1}= {2a+3b}/ {2a-3b} $

যোজন বিয়োজন করিয়া পাই
বা, ${(x^2+2x+1)+(x^2-2x+1)} /{(x^2+2x+1)-(x^2-2x+1)}= {(2a+3b) + (2a-3b)}/ {(2a+3b)-(2a-3b)} $

বা, ${x^2+2x+1+x^2-2x+1} /{x^2+2x+1-x^2+2x-1)}= {2a+3b + 2a-3b}/ {2a+3b-2a+3b} $

বা, ${2x^2+2} /{4x}= {4a}/ {6b} $
